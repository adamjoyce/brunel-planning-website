About Us

Rory Joyce

Independent Planning Consultant

BA(Hons) MSc CEnv FRICS MRTPI FRGS

Rory Joyce is the Principal of Brunel Planning.

He has given Strategic Planning advice on a wide range of commercial, residential and infrastructure developments to both public and private sector clients for 35 years.

He was the President of the Planning and Development division of the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors and on the RICS Planning & Development Council for 10 years.

He was a Specialist Adviser to the House of Commons Environment Select Committee for five years. He has been a member of the Oxford Joint Planning Law Conference Committee since 1996. He was a member of the Expert Panel for the Airports Commission 2014-2015.

Kate Espezel

Independent Planning Consultant

BA(Hons) DipTP MRTPI

Kate Espezel is a Consultant at Brunel Planning.

She has worked with Brunel Planning for four years and has been a town planning consultant for sixteen years, having worked previously at GVA Grimley, Drivers Jonas and Deloitte.

Kate's professional experience includes involvement in a range of residential, office, retail and mixed use projects for both private and public sector clients. Her experience includes undertaking planning appraisals, advising on the development potential of sites, submitting and negotiating planning applications and preparing evidence for planning appeals.

Julia Chowings

Independent Planning Consultant

BA(Hons) DipTP MRTPI

Julia Chowings is a Consultant at Brunel Planning.

She has over 20 years years experience advising public and private sector clients on a wide range of commercial, residential and infrastructure projects.

Julia was previously a Partner at both Deloitte Real Estate and Drivers Jonas. Julia sat on the British Property Federation (BPF) Industrial Committee.

Julia's clients include, Pension Funds, Developers, Housebuilders, Warehouse and Logistic operators, National Institutions, Education and Healthcare providers and Infrastructure operators.

Julia has advised developers on many major city centre regeneration projects. She has provided strategic advice to Pension funds across their property portfolios. Julia has also advised on airport related development including the UK Search and Rescue Service. Julia also has expert witness experience.
